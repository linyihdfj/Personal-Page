# Introduction

This note tracks key papers around efficient multimodal learning.

## Paper

### title

Paper A: Efficient Multimodal Pretraining

### arxiv-link

https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.00001

### hallucination-translation-link

https://example.com/hallucination-translation/2401-00001

### rating

4.5

### core-idea

Use curriculum-based token routing to reduce expensive cross-modal attention while preserving alignment quality.

### abstract

The paper proposes an efficient multimodal pretraining strategy combining sparse routing and curriculum schedules, yielding strong downstream transfer with lower FLOPs.

### hallucination-translation-link

### rating

4

### core-idea

Transfer teacher-level cross-modal reasoning to compact students through structured distillation losses.

A distillation framework for vision-language models that uses region-text matching constraints and progressive supervision for smaller student models.
